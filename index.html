ABOUT YOUR INSTRUCTOR

Meet Sakshi Pandey

Sakshi Pandey is a passionate and dedicated yoga instructor committed to helping individuals achieve better physical health, mental well-being, flexibility, and inner peace through authentic yoga practices.

She is NET Qualified, holds a Master's Degree in Yoga, and has successfully completed Teacher Training (TTT) certified by Yoga Alliance, an internationally recognized certification for yoga professionals.

As a former AIUC (All India Inter University) Player, she brings discipline, strength, and a deep understanding of fitness into every yoga session.

She is professionally certified in Hatha Yoga and Ashtanga Yoga, offering classes suitable for beginners as well as experienced practitioners.

👧 Kids Yoga Specialist

1 Year Teaching Experience at Kendriya Vidyalaya

Experienced in conducting engaging and age-appropriate yoga sessions for children, helping improve flexibility, concentration, posture, confidence, and overall physical and mental well-being through fun and interactive practices.
